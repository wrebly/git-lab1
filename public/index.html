Our Story

A Legacy of Flavour

7+ Years

Where Every Meal is a Celebration

Nestled near M.S. Pai Petrol Pump in Adyar, Mangalore, Tandoor Kitchen has been serving authentic flavours for over seven years. From sizzling tandoori specials to fragrant biryanis and crispy Chinese starters, our kitchen brings together the best of South Indian, North Indian and Chinese cuisines under one roof.

Our master chefs craft every dish with the finest ingredients, time-honoured recipes and a passion that has kept our guests coming back for years. Whether you dine in or order for delivery, we promise an experience that delights every sense.
